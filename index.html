About Us

Samaj Vikas Sansthan is a non-governmental organization dedicated to the development of society through education, health, awareness, and social welfare activities.For more than 34 years, we have actively worked on water conservation, climate change awareness, and the expansion of organic farming practices across 16 districts of Western Uttar Pradesh. Under the leadership of Dr. K. K. Tomar, a dedicated environmental activist from Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, we strive for sustainable and community-driven development.

Our Work

We work in areas such as education for underprivileged children, health camps, women empowerment, environmental awareness, and community development programs.
